A city remade

London Under Watch imagines the city as a controlled metropolis of surveillance, propaganda, and resistance. Towers rise over transit systems, public screens dictate behaviour, and hidden acts of defiance survive in the gaps between authority.

Each print captures a different fragment of that world — a district, a structure, a warning, or a moment of quiet rebellion. Together, they form a portrait of a city reshaped by control.
