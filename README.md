# Cities Under Watch

Cities Under Watch is a Django e-commerce platform for dystopian digital city posters, combining narrative worldbuilding, editorial design, and downloadable are sales.

---

## Project Overview

Cities Under Watch is an original concept for a digital poster store centred on fictional and reimagined cities shaped by surveillance, authoritarian architecture, and quiet resistance. The platform allows users to browse themed collections, explore the context behind each print, purchase downloadable artwork, and access their digital orders through their personal account area.

The goal is to create an authentic digital art brand and e-commerce experience that feels publishable, immersive, and commercially viable.

---

## Project Background

Cities Under Watch was developed as part of the Level 5 Diploma in Web Application Development.

---

## Project Goals

### User Goals
- Browse dystopian city poster collections
- View detailed product information before purchase
- Purchase downloadable digital prints securely
- Access purchased downloads through an account area
- Understand the concept and world behind each collection

### Site Owner Goals
- Present a strong, original digital art brand
- Sell digital poster products through a believable e-commerce experience
- Organise products into themed city collections
- Encourage users to browse, purchase, and return for future collections
- Maintain a manageable admin workflow for products, collections, and orders

---

## Target Audience

The target audience for Cities Under Watch includes:
- people interested in dystopian visual culture
- fans of speculative fiction and graphic-novel-inspired artwork
- buyers of digital wall art and printable posters
- users drawn to themes of surveillance, architecture, power, and resistance
- creative individuals looking for statement artwork for home or studio spaces

---

## Project Identity

### Clearly Original Concept
Cities Under Watch is an original digital poster e-commerce project built around a fictional narrative world. It uses a custom concept, custom content, custom visual identity, and custom page structure to create a distinct brand and shopping experience.

### Real Target Audience and Purpose
The site is designed for users who want to browse, purchase, and download digital poster artwork tied to a strong conceptual theme. It is structured as a believable commercial product with a clear e-commerce purpose.

### Consistent Theme
The project theme is carried across:
- branding
- typography
- colour palette
- page layouts
- product copy
- collection structure
- supporting context sections

### Real-Store Feel
The goal is for the site to feel like a genuine independent digital poster store rather than a student exercise.
